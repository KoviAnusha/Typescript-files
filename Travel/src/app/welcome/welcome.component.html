DBS Omega is India’s largest online bus ticketing platform that has transformed bus travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using buses. Founded in 2000, DBS Omega is part of India’s leading online travel company. By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits, DBS omega has served over 8 million customers. DBS omega has a global presence with operations across Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Taiwan apart from India.
